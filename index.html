Wayfarer

A location based website that allows members to review places they've visited

Wayfarer is a platform designed to share and inspire traveling. As a user-based app, Wayfarer offers unique travel suggestions from users all over the world.

By creating an account, members are able to drop pins on the map to track and save their favorite travel spots, upload pictures, and share travel ideas with the Wayfarer community.

Each pin on the map is a unique travel post, created by a Warfarer member. Clicking on a pin will display the post's content.

Burger App

A burger making app made using Node, Sequalize, mysql, and Handlebars.

Eat-Da-Burger! is a restaurant app that lets users input the names of burgers they'd like to eat. Whenever a user submits a burger's name, the app will display the burger on the left side of the page -- waiting to be devoured. Each burger in the waiting area also has a `Eat It!` button. When the user clicks it, the burger will move to the right side of the page. The app will store every burger in a database, whether devoured or not.
